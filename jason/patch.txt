Steps:
1. Make single jar with all in one
2. Copy jar to <JASON>/lib/aorta and <JASON>/bin/jedit/jars
3. Copy aorta.xml & aorta-metamodel.xml to <JASON>/bin/jedit/modes
4. Add entries for .aorta and .mm in <JASON>/bin/jedit/modes/catalog
5. Add line: "infrastructure.AORTA=aorta.jason.infra.AortaFactory" to <USER-DIR>/.jason/user.properties
6. Add lines: "mode.aorta.sidekick.parser=aorta" "sidekick.parser.aorta.label=aorta" to <USER-DIR>/.jedit/properties


<JASON>/lib/aorta:
- antlr
- framework
- jason
- metamodel
- jpf
- tuprolog

<JASON>/bin/jedit/jars:
- antlr
- framework
- jason
- metamodel
- jpf
- tuprolog
- jeditplugin

<JASON>/bin/jedit/modes/aorta.xml
<JASON>/bin/jedit/modes/aorta-metamodel.xml

<JASON>/bin/jedit/modes/catalog:
- add entry for .aorta
- add entry for .mm


<USER-DIR>/.jason/user.properties:
infrastructure.AORTA=aorta.jason.infra.AortaFactory

<USER-DIR>/.jedit/properties:
mode.aorta.sidekick.parser=aorta
sidekick.parser.aorta.label=aorta
